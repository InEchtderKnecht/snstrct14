Open Call

SINSTRUCT is looking for artistic
contributions enhancing the festival!

SINSTRUCT considers itself as an interregional, interdisciplinary, platform for cross-cultural interaction between various participants.
We have a strong focus on audiences for subcultural art forms, a support of the creation of new networks and artists willing to present their art off the ordinary, beaten paths.

Sinstruct Festival, in its 3rd year now, will be taking place in the Southern Tyrolean alps on 1500m above sea level near to the village Unsere Liebe Frau im Walde and will be held from Aug 7th till Aug 10th 2014.

Apart from the deep forest and the bloomy meadows, small caves, glades and a bunker are located on the area. We want to encourage you to start there and explore new ways of perception for the unheard and the unseen and push the diversification of art.
It's up to you to give a performance or a workshop, to build sound and video installations or sculptures.
