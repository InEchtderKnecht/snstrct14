Open Call

Wanna get involved
in our arts-culture festival Sinstruct?



SINSTRUCT understands itself as an interregional, interdisciplinary, platform for cross-cultural interaction between various fields of artistic expression.

We have a strong focus on supporting the creation of new networks and audiences for subcultural artforms and artists producing art off the beaten path of institutionalized and exclusive high-cultural or mainstream channels.



Sinstruct Festival, in its 3rd year now, will be taking place in the Southern Tyrolean alps near to Unsere Liebe Frau im Walde and will be held from Aug 7th till Aug 10th 2014.

We provide the stage for all you likeminded creatives out there from various fields of artistic expression.

So now everyone feeling with us in our approach of building new ways of perception for the unheard and the unseen, pushing the diversivication of art and its audience or creating contrasts to corporate cultural production, please feel invited to follow our OPEN CALL …



Get involved now and send your vision, concept, portfolio, web-link of further references to opencall(at)sinstruct.com.
Since we have limited capacity and do the programming on our own, we cannot guarantee to accept every suggestion to be a part of this year’s Sinstruct festival.

If you feel the urge to support our vision, please feel free to republish this Open Call or forward it to any artist you think, might be a promising addition to Sinstruct Festival.
